How It Works

Three Steps. One Powerful Output.

No accounts, no installs, no complicated setup. Just open the app and go.

1

Capture

Hit record during a live sermon, paste a transcript you already have, or drop in a YouTube link. The app handles the rest.

2

Analyze

Claude AI reads the full transcript and identifies the structure, themes, key moments, and scriptural connections.

3

Use & Share

Copy any section, download the full analysis, or share it to OneDrive, Google Drive, or any app on your phone.

What You Get

A Complete Sermon Toolkit — Instantly

Every analysis produces seven distinct deliverables, each designed to serve a different purpose.

Elevator Pitch

Tell Someone About It

A compelling 1-2 sentence summary you can text to a friend, post on social media, or use to invite someone to listen.

Main Points

Structured Summary

4-6 main points with sub-points, capturing the full arc and argument of the sermon in an outline you can study from.

Sermon Arc

7 Key Quotes

Direct quotes spanning the full message — opening to closing — so you relive the journey, not just the highlights.

Small Group

Group Study Guide

7 discussion questions with an opening, subpoint questions, and a reflective closer. Pick any 3-4 and you have a solid study.

Scripture

Verse References

2-3 Bible verses chosen specifically for how they speak to this sermon's themes — not generic, but precisely matched.

Response

Prayer & Conclusions

A heartfelt prayer drawn from the sermon's specific themes, plus key takeaways that crystallize the message.
